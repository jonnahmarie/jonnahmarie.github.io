About Me

My name is Jonnah Marie. I am a Los Angeles native. This past August, I recently moved back to California after nearly 3 years of living in New Zealand after having earned my Bachelor of Science in Zoology and Ecology there.

Despite my education, I currently work as a graphic designer but hope to work my way into front end development. I have worked a wide range of jobs from entertainment payroll to veterinary assistance, however, since my late childhood years, I have always enjoyed the aspect of creating aesthetically pleasing visual web applications with plain text lines of code and am still to the day often inspired by beautiful web pages like they are works of art. This interest has led me to the decision of enrolling in the UCLA Coding Bootcamp.

I like tech and have just recently built my first PC. I am a big fan of DIY projects, including yarn crafts such as knitting and crocheting. My other hobbies include cooking and baking, watching Korean drama shows, playing games, and reading. My go-to TV networks I cycle through are HGTV and the Food Network. However, I also thoroughly enjoy pop culture and am a big fan of Harry Potter, Star Wars, The Lord of the Rings and more.
